Welcome to kuberspatiotemporal
==============================

A package to experiment with probabilistic,
heterogeneous mixture models for risk analysis.
Based on `(Kimura et al. 2011)`_ by DeepMind's `Arnaud Doucet`_ and
`(Krueger et al. 2018)`_, and maybe also `(Neal and Hinton 1998)`_.
